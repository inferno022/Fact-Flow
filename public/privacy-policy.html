Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 5, 2026

Fact Flow ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Email address and username when you create an account
  • Usage Data: Facts you've viewed, liked, and saved; your progress and achievements
  • Device Information: Device type and operating system for app optimization

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and personalize your fact feed
  • To track your progress, streaks, and achievements
  • To send push notifications (with your permission)
  • To improve our app and user experience

Data Storage

Your data is securely stored using Supabase, a trusted cloud database provider. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information.

Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers for ad personalization. See Google's Privacy Policy
  • Google Gemini AI: For generating educational facts. No personal data is shared with this service
  • Supabase: For secure data storage and authentication

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt out of push notifications
  • Request data portability

Children's Privacy

Fact Flow is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. The educational content is family-friendly.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You can delete your account at any time from the app settings, which will remove all associated data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app.
